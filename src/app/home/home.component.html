About Me

I am a 3rd year computer science major at the University of Cincinnati currently pursuing my Masters degree. I have a passion for building applications other people can use to connect with each other. I'm a big advocate for the future of web apps and want to do what I can to help the web do everything native can, on every platform. I usually split my working time developing for the web, and studying and solving algorithms.

When developing for the web I focus on building and experimenting with Progressive Web Apps and learning RxJS and Angular 2. I'm fairly involved with the Angular community and spend a good bit of time in the Gitter chat room.

I like to study algorithms & discrete mathematics, and spend a lot of time solving problems on online judges such as:

  • SPOJ
  • Leetcode
  • Google Code Jam
Problem solving can be, addicting, and if I find a problem whose answer consists of some neat symmetry I might write a short post about it.

I like to play the piano and run in my free time. On opposite day I'm a right-handed spider enthusiast and future chemist.
